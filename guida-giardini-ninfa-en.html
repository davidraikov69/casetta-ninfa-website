Complete Guide to the Garden of Ninfa

Considered by the New York Times to be the most beautiful and romantic garden in the world, the Garden of Ninfa is a unique monumental oasis, located just minutes from our B&B. A magical place where medieval ruins, crystal-clear streams, and lush vegetation merge into an unforgettable landscape. Here is our complete guide to planning your visit.

Booking and Tickets: A Crucial Step

Visiting the Garden of Ninfa is an exclusive experience, and to protect its delicate ecosystem, admissions are limited to specific opening dates throughout the year. Online booking is mandatory.

  • Where to book: Tickets can only be purchased on the official website www.giardinodininfa.eu. Beware of other channels.
  • When to book: We recommend booking weeks, or even months, in advance, especially for weekends in April and May when the blooms are at their peak.
  • Cost: The ticket price includes a contribution to the Roffredo Caetani Foundation, which is responsible for the garden's maintenance and preservation.

How to Get There from B&B Casetta Ninfa

Convenience is one of our strengths. From B&B Casetta Ninfa, you can reach the garden entrance in less than **10 minutes by car**. Simply follow the signs for "Giardino di Ninfa." You will find a large free car park upon arrival.

What to See and Tips for Your Visit

The guided tour lasts about an hour and will take you through the most picturesque spots in the garden. Don't miss:

  • The Ninfa River, with its incredibly transparent waters.
  • The Roman-era bridges and the ruins of the Church of Santa Maria Maggiore.
  • The blooming Japanese cherry trees in spring.
  • The variety of maple trees, which offer spectacular colors in autumn.

Tip: Wear comfortable shoes, as the path is on natural trails. Picnics are not allowed inside, but you will find food stalls nearby.
